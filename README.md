# Coffee Roasters

A modern, responsive coffee subscription service website that showcases artisan coffee selections and offers personalized delivery plans.

## Overview

Coffee Roasters is a web application designed to connect coffee enthusiasts with premium artisan coffees from around the world. The platform features a curated collection of expertly roasted coffee beans, each with unique flavor profiles and origins.

## Key Features

- **Coffee Collection**: Discover four distinct coffee varieties:
  - Gran Espresso: Light and flavorful blend with cocoa and black pepper notes
  - Planalto: Brazilian dark roast with rich, velvety body and hints of fruits and nuts
  - Piccollo: Mild and smooth blend featuring toasted almond and dried cherry notes
  - Danche: Ethiopian hand-harvested blend with vibrant fruit notes

- **Subscription Service**: Create personalized coffee delivery plans tailored to your preferences and schedule

- **Premium Benefits**:
  - Best quality artisan coffees from top roasters worldwide
  - Exclusive subscriber benefits including 30% off first shipment
  - Free shipping with guaranteed peak freshness

## Technology

Built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3 with responsive design, and Bootstrap 5 for cross-device compatibility. The site features a clean, professional design with custom typography and smooth user interactions.

## Purpose

To make exceptional coffee accessible to everyone by bridging the gap between specialty coffee roasters and coffee lovers, delivering curated selections directly to customers' doors on their preferred schedule.
